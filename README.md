# CivicSphere 🌍

CivicSphere is a civic engagement and awareness platform designed to educate and motivate citizens, especially youth, about civic responsibilities and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

The platform transforms civic learning into an interactive and engaging experience through quizzes, community participation, action tracking, and gamification.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 🧠 Civic Quiz Module
  Interactive quizzes about civic rights, duties, governance, and SDGs.

* 📍 Civic Action Tracker
  Encourages users to complete real-world civic activities and track contributions.

* 🎙️ CivicVoice Community Wall
  Users can report civic issues, share ideas, and collaborate on solutions.

* 🏆 Leaderboards & Rewards
  Gamified system with badges, XP points, and rankings to encourage participation.

* 🌐 SDG Explorer
  Learn about the 17 Sustainable Development Goals and how local actions contribute to global impact.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Promote civic responsibility and awareness.
* Encourage community participation and social impact.
* Connect everyday civic actions with global sustainability goals.
* Make civic education engaging through gamification.

## 🌍 SDGs Addressed

This project contributes towards:

* SDG 4 – Quality Education
* SDG 11 – Sustainable Cities and Communities
* SDG 12 – Responsible Consumption and Production
* SDG 13 – Climate Action
* SDG 16 – Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions
* SDG 17 – Partnerships for the Goals
